Hobbies

I like to consider myself a man of many interests. So here are a few activities I like to engage in, but in the interest of full disclosure, some of them may shock you. This list is presented in no defined order:

  • Nature, wildlife, and landscape photography
  • Cooking (I especially enjoy trying out new recipes)
  • Baking (I know, some people consider baking and cooking to be the same, but I like to make the distinction)
  • Reading (I like novels and thrillers)
  • Singing
  • Conversations (erm, productive ones... I do not enjoy arguments)
  • Hiking (alone or in a group, just make sure to keep up with my fast pace)
  • Faith (from studying my Bible to praying, I feel centered when I spend time with God)
  • Movies (whether its watching them or having conversations about them, I am a bonafide movie enthusiast!)
  • Eating (I am something of a foodie, although, I am not known for my great appetite)
  • Sleeping (because at my core, I am an introvert whose idea of heaven is laying on my bed 24/7 with absolutely nothing to do)

So if I seem to be your cup of tea, why don't you reach out to me on my contact page? I promise I don't bite.
